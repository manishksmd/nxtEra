Hey.

NxtEra Food & Dairy is a private limited company founded in July 2018. We have started with an aim of producing high quality and nutritious milk. We at this company share the passion of dairy industry and are dedicated to our families, our employees, farmers and consumers. At very young age we have started gaining confidence of our customers.

NxtEra Food & Dairy manufacture, market and sells milk and milk products under Gomedh brand.

What I Do

Mission:

To become a quality Producer and Supplier of Milk and Milk Products with advanced technological services to consumers to achieve customer satisfaction. Provide our customers with the cleanest and healthiest milk, for which the focus on nutrition starts from the animals themselves, thus leading to more nutritional milk and milk products. NxtEra Food & Dairy will improve living standard of farmers, employees and community year after year by benchmarking against world class standards in Dairy Industry.

 Vision

To become leaders in the Indian Dairy Industry, while retaining our Indian traditions and family values.

 Goal

To provide all kinds of Milk and Milk products to customers at their doorstep accessible from all available channel at all time. To provide a quality service with latest technology in dairy, consulting to new entrepreneurs, farmers and communities.
